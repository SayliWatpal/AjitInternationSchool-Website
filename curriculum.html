Curriculum

A Balanced Academic Journey

Our curriculum is designed to build knowledge, skills, confidence, and values.

Academics

Courses of Study

Nursery, LKG & UKG: Moral Instruction, English, Mathematics, General Awareness, Arts

Std I–III: English, Mathematics, EVS, Hindi, Marathi, Computer, GK, PT & Arts

Std IV–V: English, Mathematics, General Science, Social Studies, Hindi, Marathi, Computer, GK, PT & Arts

Std VI–VIII: English, Mathematics, General Science, Social Studies, Hindi, Marathi, Computer, PT & Arts

Std IX–X: English, Mathematics, General Science, Social Studies, Hindi, Marathi, Computer, PT

Std XI–XII (Science): English, Marathi, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, Physics

School Uniform

Pre-Primary: Navy blue skirt/shorts with light pink shirt, white socks, black shoes

Std I–X: Navy blue shirt, grey jacket, navy blue pants, school belt

PT Uniform: House color T-shirt with black track pants

Note: Purchase uniforms from authorized dealers only.

Examinations

  • First PT, First Term, Second PT, Third PT, Second Term
  • Term Exam: 80 marks theory + 20 marks project
  • PT Exams: 20 marks each

Promotion Rules

  • Passing marks: 33% (Std I–X), 35% (Std XI–XII)
  • Failing in English → No promotion
  • Students failing twice → must discontinue

Attendance & Leave

  • 75% attendance mandatory
  • Leave requires parent application
  • Medical certificate needed after illness

Discipline Policy

  • No abusive language, bullying, or misconduct
  • No drugs, weapons, or harmful activities
  • Violation may lead to suspension or expulsion

Extra-Curricular Activities

  • Sports Day, Annual Day, Science Exhibition
  • Speech, Drawing, Poetry Competitions
  • Yoga Day, Independence Day, Republic Day

Assignments

  • Submit work on time
  • Maintain notebook index
  • Parents should monitor progress
