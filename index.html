About Us

Where Education Means All-Round Development

We believe every child deserves a learning environment that builds knowledge, confidence, character, and creativity.

Shaping Responsible and Confident Learners

Ajit International School is dedicated to offering quality education in a caring and inspiring environment. We focus on academic growth along with moral values, communication, discipline, and personality development.

With experienced teachers and a student-centered approach, we help children prepare for the future with strength and integrity.

Our Inspiration

A freedom fighter and former MLA of the Malkapur Assembly Constituency is our true source of inspiration in helping rural communities overcome illiteracy. He was one of the pioneers of India’s freedom struggle and sacrificed his life selflessly, becoming an enduring symbol of patriotism for all of us.

He strongly believed that without education, society cannot progress or become truly civilized. Guided by this vision, he established Yashawant High School at Chandur Biswa in 1963. The school became a valuable opportunity for students in rural areas to receive quality education.

Later, in 2004, Ajit Convent was started to help rural children learn through the English medium. In 2017, the Arts and Science Junior College, attached to Yashawant High School, was also established. Today, we proudly have two successfully functioning ISO-certified schools.

With over 58 years of experience in the field of education, and with the inspiration of Late Jalamsinghji Ingle, we continue to move forward on the path of success with the close cooperation of parents, students, and the community.
